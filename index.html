Recipe of Rice with Chicken

Hi my friends, I want to tell you how to cook very tasty rice with chicken. I will try to teach you how to cook it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs or breast — 500 g (cubed)
  • Rice — 1 cup
  • Onion — 1 medium (cubed)
  • Carrot — 1 medium
  • Garlic — 3 cloves (minced)
  • Tomato (or 2 tbsp tomato paste) — 1 medium
  • Oil — 2–3 tablespoons
  • Water — add until 0.5–1 cm above ingredients
  • Spices: salt, black pepper, paprika, fresh parsley or cilantro — for garnish

Instructions

  1. Clean and cut all your vegetables, wash your rice (3–4 times in cold water until clear) and cut your chicken.
  2. First you need to fry chicken, add some oil in the frying pan and cook it until you get a golden crust.
  3. Put the chicken on a plate, leave the oil in the pan.
  4. Add vegetables to the oil and fry for 3–5 minutes, then add chicken and tomato paste, a little water, and spices. Cook 8–10 minutes with lid closed.
  5. Add washed rice on top (don’t stir). Pour water until 0.5–1 cm above. Salt the water. Cook 10–15 minutes and check the rice. The main thing: do not stir while cooking, otherwise the rice will be overcooked.
